Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Booking & Scheduling

How do I book a service?

You can book a service through our website by clicking the "Book Now" button, selecting your preferred service package, choosing a date and time, and completing the booking form. You can also call us directly at +880 1234 567 890.

What are your operating hours?

We operate Monday through Sunday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 12 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. Cancellations within 12 hours may incur a fee.

Services & Pricing

What services do you offer?

We offer three main service packages: Basic Wash (exterior and basic interior), Premium Wash (complete exterior and interior cleaning), and Detailing (professional detailing with paint protection). We service both cars and motorcycles.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies by service type and vehicle type. Basic wash starts at ৳199 for bikes and ৳299 for cars. Premium wash is ৳399 for bikes and ৳599 for cars. Detailing services start at ৳899 for bikes and ৳1299 for cars.

Do you provide mobile services?

Yes, we provide mobile services and come to your location. You just need to provide a suitable area with access to water and ensure your vehicle is accessible.

Payment & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bKash, and Nagad payments. Payment is due upon completion of the service.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer refunds for eligible cases. If you're not satisfied with our service, please contact us within 24 hours of service completion. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Service Process

How long does a service take?

Service duration varies by package: Basic wash takes 30-45 minutes, Premium wash takes 45-60 minutes, and Detailing takes 2-3 hours.

What should I do before the service?

Please remove all personal belongings from your vehicle, ensure it's accessible, and provide a suitable area with water access for our workers.

Are your workers trained and insured?

Yes, all our workers are professionally trained and insured. They follow strict safety protocols and use high-quality cleaning products.

Support & Contact

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact us via phone at +880 1234 567 890, email at support@autocleanbd.com, or through the contact form on our website. We respond within 24 hours.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the greater Dhaka area, including Bashundhara, Gulshan, Banani, Dhanmondi, and surrounding areas. We're expanding to other cities soon.
